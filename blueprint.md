
# Blueprint: Kim Boa's Portfolio Website

## Overview

A personal portfolio website for Kim Boa, a university student, to showcase her skills, projects, and contact information.

## Design & Style

*   **Aesthetics:** Modern, clean, and professional.
*   **Color Palette:** A vibrant and energetic palette with a mix of neutral and accent colors.
*   **Typography:** Expressive and readable fonts to create a visual hierarchy.
*   **Texture:** A subtle noise texture for the background to add a premium feel.
*   **Shadows:** Multi-layered drop shadows to create a sense of depth and lift UI elements.
*   **Interactivity:** Interactive elements with a "glow" effect on hover.

## Features

*   **Header:** A prominent header with Kim Boa's name and title.
*   **About Me Section:** A section to introduce Kim Boa and her academic background.
*   **Projects Section:** A section to showcase her projects using custom web components for a modular design.
*   **Skills Section:** A list of her skills.
*   **Contact Section:** A section with her contact information.
*   **Responsive Design:** The website will be fully responsive and accessible on all devices.
